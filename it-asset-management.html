From the moment an asset enters your organization until its final day, IT asset tagging ensures complete visibility, accountability, and control every step of the way.

IT asset tagging involves assigning each asset a unique identifier containing important information such as its type, assigned owner, current status, location, and usage record. Whether applied as a physical label or a digital tag, this system allows organizations to easily monitor and manage assets from acquisition to disposal.

What is an IT Tag?

It may sound straightforward, but defining it clearly helps avoid confusion. An IT tag is a unique label—physical or digital—assigned to an IT asset to identify it and store key details. These tags are specific to technology-related assets such as laptops, servers, networking equipment, or software licenses, and they form a specialized category within the broader concept of asset tags.
